Meet Corey and Kristen

Corey and Kristen

Corey started pastoring in his mid-thirties and says that was a good thing because of his immaturity he might have been more harm than good to people before that. One of the smartest things he has ever done was marry Kristen in 1988. When you spend some time with Corey, his love for history, humor, and people quickly become evident.

Kristen is a third-generation minister and spent much of her childhood in the jungles of Brazil where her dad was a missionary pilot. She has a passion to see people thrive in healthy relationships. She has been married to the love of her life Corey for almost 30 years. They have three grown children and one granddaughter.

Because we value authenticity, our motto is: "No perfect people allowed."
